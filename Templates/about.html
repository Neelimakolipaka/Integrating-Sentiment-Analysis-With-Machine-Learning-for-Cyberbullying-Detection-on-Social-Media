Model Evaluation & Data Insights

Explore key metrics and visualizations that provide a comprehensive understanding of our dataset and the performance of our cyberbullying detection model.

Distribution of Data

This visualization shows how the data is distributed across different categories, helping to identify class imbalances and trends within the dataset.

Overall Word Cloud of All Cleaned Text

The word cloud highlights the most frequent and significant words in the cleaned dataset, offering insights into common themes and topics discussed.

Confusion Matrix

The confusion matrix provides a detailed breakdown of the model's predictions, showing the number of correct and incorrect classifications for each class.

Accuracy Score

Accuracy measures the overall correctness of the model by calculating the proportion of true results among the total number of cases examined.

Precision Score

Precision indicates how many of the items identified as positive by the model are actually positive, reflecting the model's ability to avoid false positives.

Recall Score

Recall measures the model's ability to find all relevant cases within a dataset, indicating how well it identifies true positives.

F1 Score

The F1 score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, providing a balanced metric that considers both false positives and false negatives.
